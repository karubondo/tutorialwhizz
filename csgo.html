CSGO Tutorial

Welcome to the CSGO tutorial! In this guide, you'll learn weapon strategies, map control, and team-based tactics to dominate matches.

Getting Started

Select your role: Entry Fragger, Support, AWPer, or Lurker. Each role has specific responsibilities during rounds. Learn movement mechanics like strafing, crouching, and peeking to gain advantages.

Weapons and Loadouts

Choose weapons based on map and strategy. Rifles like the AK-47 and M4A4 are versatile; the AWP excels at long-range pickoffs. Buy grenades and utility equipment wisely to control enemy movement and create openings.

Combat Tips

  • Use crosshair placement to minimize reaction time during firefights.
  • Master recoil patterns for accurate shooting.
  • Check corners and listen for enemy footsteps.
  • Economy management is key: know when to save or buy.

Map Knowledge

Learn callouts for each map, understand chokepoints, bombsites, and rotations. Controlling mid and high-traffic areas can change the round outcome.

Team Play

Communicate with teammates about enemy positions, utility usage, and strategies. Supporting teammates with flashes, smokes, and heals improves win rates.

Advanced Tips

  • Master grenade lineups for smokes, flashes, and molotovs.
  • Time pushes with team coordination.
  • Study enemy patterns and adapt in-game strategies.
  • Participate in competitive matches to refine decision-making under pressure.
