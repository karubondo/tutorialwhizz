Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up link in the navbar, fill in your email and password, and submit the form. Your account will be created instantly.

What if I forget my password?

Currently, there is no password recovery system. You will need to create a new account if you forget your password.

How do I track my progress?

Navigate to the Progress Tracker page to see your game statistics and progress over time.

Can I edit my profile information?

Yes, click the Profile link in the navbar to update your username, email, or other profile settings.

How do I provide feedback?

Click the Feedback link in the navbar and fill out the form to send your suggestions or report issues.
