Combat Tips

  • Prioritize high-value targets like healers or damage dealers.
  • Use ultimate abilities strategically, not impulsively.
  • Stay aware of enemy cooldowns and positioning.
  • Practice aim, movement, and ability timing for maximum efficiency.

Team Strategies

  • Communicate via voice or text to coordinate pushes and defenses.
  • Focus fire together to take down key enemies quickly.
  • Adapt hero picks based on enemy composition mid-match.
  • Control objectives and choke points instead of chasing kills.

Advanced Tips

  • Learn map layouts to use flanks and high ground effectively.
  • Counter-pick enemies with heroes that exploit weaknesses.
  • Track enemy ultimates to prevent devastating combos.
  • Analyze replays to identify mistakes and optimize strategy.
